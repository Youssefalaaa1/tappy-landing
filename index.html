Everything owners ask us.

Do my customers need to download an app?
No. They tap your NFC card or scan the QR and they're in. Earning starts immediately. The Tappy app is a bonus for customers who want all their cards, history, and rewards in one place.
What hardware do I need?
None. We ship pre-programmed NFC cards for each of your branches. Just place one by the register. No terminal, no tablet, no monthly hardware fee. Your phone handles staff scanning through the Tappy app.
I run an online business. Does Tappy work for me?
Yes. Online businesses use QR codes and links instead of a counter card. Drop them in order confirmations or packages, and customers earn the same way. Your shipping address stays private; customers never see it.
How does per-branch pricing work?
Each plan includes a number of branches in the base price. Need more? Add them for a flat monthly rate per branch: $9 on Starter, $7 on Growth, $5 on Pro. Pro has no branch cap at all.
Stamp cards or points: which should I pick?
Stamp cards ("buy 8, get 1 free") are perfect for cafés, barbers, and bakeries. Simple and addictive. Points and tiers suit restaurants and retail where spend varies. You pick per business, and you can fine-tune everything later.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no minimums. Your 14-day trial is free, and after that you can cancel whenever you like from the app.
